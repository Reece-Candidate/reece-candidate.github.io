About Services

Personal Injury

If you have been injured as a result of negligence or by the intentional conduct of another then you may have a claim under Georgia law for monetary damages. Examples include being rear-ended in a car collision, being struck by a car that ran a red light, being injured at a business establishment where a substance on the floor caused the slip and fall, being detained or arrested without just cause, being wrongfully accused of a crime, being injured by a doctor or medical facility as a result of medical negligence.

Probate, Administration and Wills

  • Probate is the process of having a will admitted to probate.
  • Administration of an estate is the process of administering a person’s estate who did not have a will.
  • Wills are documents that set forth how a person’s property is to be distributed after that person’s death.

Estate Planning and Trusts

  • Estate planning is a process to determine the best way for an individual to provide for the distribution of that individual’s assets both during and after that individual’s life.
  • Trusts are used to for a multitude of purposes. These include estate planning, planning for an individual’s possible disability, protecting the assets of individuals who lack sufficient ability to do so for themselves.
